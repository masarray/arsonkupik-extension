# Supporting ArSonKuPik Development

ArSonKuPik is independently developed and free to use. Optional voluntary support helps fund audio testing, browser compatibility work, maintenance, documentation, and continued DSP improvements.

## Current scope

The first support phase is Indonesia-only through an official static merchant QRIS displayed on the project website. The extension contains only a user-initiated link to that page. It does not process payments, receive transaction events, identify supporters, unlock features, or send analytics.

## Product principles

- Support is optional and never required to start or continue audio processing.
- No core feature, preset, audio quality level, or support channel is conditioned on payment.
- The support page never opens automatically.
- No transaction or supporter data is stored by the extension.
- No affiliate link, advertising SDK, tracking pixel, or payment SDK is bundled.
- The public wording uses “support development” rather than implying a charitable or tax-deductible donation.
