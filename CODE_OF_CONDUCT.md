# Community Code of Conduct

ArSonKuPik welcomes respectful technical collaboration.

- Discuss ideas and code, not personal attributes.
- Be specific, patient, and constructive in reviews.
- Credit prior work and disclose conflicts of interest.
- Do not publish private data, security details, or confidential material.
- Avoid harassment, threats, discrimination, and intentionally disruptive behavior.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove contributions that violate these expectations and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its community.
